Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join NEXUS?

NEXUS is open to all students regardless of their major or year of study. We welcome beginners and experienced individuals alike!

Is there a membership fee?

No, joining NEXUS is completely free! Some specific events or workshops may have a small fee to cover materials.

How often are meetings held?

We hold general meetings every two weeks. Working groups and project teams meet more frequently based on their schedules.

Do I need prior experience?

Not at all! We offer workshops and mentorship for beginners. The only requirement is curiosity and willingness to learn.
